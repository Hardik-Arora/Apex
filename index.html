Our Services

At Apex Control Systems, we treat every client like family, ensuring quality and satisfaction. We offer cost-effective services in installation, networking, and system integration, tailored to meet your security needs with care and expertise.

Doorlocks & Biometrics

With Apex, ensure security at every entrance. Our premium door locks and biometric systems capture every thumbprint, keeping your premises secure. With top-quality products, we guarantee that every movement around your property is recorded, so you can stay safe, always.

EPABX

Want to connect with your colleague instantly? With our wide range of telecom products and intercom services, we ensure seamless communication across your organization, streamlining workflow with just a tap of a button.

Fire and Intrusion Alarms

With our powerful alarm systems and hooters, every breach is met with swift action, ensuring thieves are caught. Our top-quality fire alarms detect hazards early, raising awareness before fires spread, keeping your property and loved ones safe.

Home Automation

Home automation becomes effortless with our expert installation. With just a tap or remote, transform your home into a smart, relaxing space that always sets the perfect vibe—whether for leisure or a lively party atmosphere.

CCTV surveillance

We watch over what the human eye can't see. Our high-quality HD cameras provide round-the-clock surveillance, capturing clear footage day and night to prevent incidents and protect lives and property.

Solar Panels

We believe in sustainable energy, offering cost-effective solar panel installation, along with reliable support and maintenance.

Installations and Networking

We earn our customers' trust through skilled installation of delicate, high-value products, minimizing time and resource wastage. We handle a wide range of network cables, including Cat6, EPABX, CCTV, and power cables, ensuring seamless connectivity and reliability.

Annual Maintenance Contract

Great products deserve exceptional service. Our world-class AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) ensures your systems remain in top condition, maximizing performance and extending lifespan, while keeping your technology fully optimized and reliable.

FAQ

+

How much will it cost?

To get a better idea of the cost of what you want to build, give us a quick phone call. We’ll ask you a few questions about the area and location of the site, your security needs etc. If you’re still interested, we’ll come to your place of business and come up with a firm quote.

+

How long it takes to secure my place?

The time limit of any assignment is normally dictated by the client. If you have any time limit in mind we will attempt to assemble it for you. The generic delay the completion of a site is caused by external factors such as electrical wiring issues, absence of required devices and gadgets.

+

What all brands will you use or suggest?

We use top and premium quality brands like Hikvision, Sony, Samsung, GE, Honeywell, Ahuja, Bosch to name a few. Brand selection depends upon the budget and key requirements of the client. Based on that, our team prepares a suite of brands we suggest to fulfill all your needs.

+

Can you help in my current site?

Yes. Give us a call, and explain us your needs. Wait for our professionals to pay a visit at your site to analyse the situation and Voila! You'll start noticing the wonders we can make at your site

+

When do I pay?

For most projects, equal payments are made at the start, midway, and the end of the project, but we can work with you to set up a schedule that meets your needs. We understand that this is a big investment and want to help you budget for the expense in whatever way possible.
